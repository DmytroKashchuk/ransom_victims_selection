Quick guide
Odds Ratio (OR) answers: "Are exposed subjects more likely to have the outcome than non-exposed?"
• Exposure = the suspected risk factor (e.g. sector, uses_Tech).
• Outcome = the event you measure (e.g. hacked, ransomed) — must be binary 0/1.
• Positive value = which value of the column counts as "yes". Leave blank if the column is already 0/1 / yes/no / true/false.
• OR > 1 risk factor · OR < 1 protective · CI includes 1 → not significant · p < 0.05 → significant.
If your exposure has more than 2 categories (e.g. sector = finance / healthcare / retail / government) use the By Category tab.
